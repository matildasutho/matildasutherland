Welcome to my personal site. A place where, like a garden, I forage and drop in a collection of things I find interesting and nice, and where I can also share some of my projects and research too. A little corner of the internet I've created for myself, where the rain waters the garden.

As for who I am and what I do, my name is Cristina and I'm a 26 year-old artist, curator, consultant and researcher living and working in Melbourne/Naarm on Woiworung and Boonwurrung country. My explorations in XR, live-AV projections, spatial design, installation, photography and writing are all informed by my love for nature and background in geography with emphases in ecological ethics, more-than-human relations and climate communication. In January 2020 I founded TERRAIN Projects, an initiative that creates playful physical and digital spaces to remind humans that they are embedded in a more-than-human world.

Some of my ideas, work, approaches, workshops and broader visions so far have been shared with Transitions Film Festival, Real World VR, Holland Festival (NL), NGV Art Book Fair, Melbourne Design Week, FIBER (NL), RMIT University, Melbourne Music Week, the Geography Teachers Association of Victoria, Melbourne Climate Futures Wattle Fellowship, Newkind Social Justice Conference, Hmmm Podcast, and Good Sport Magazine.

When I'm not thinking about nature and trying to articulate it in various ways, I like to spend my time offline and outside, hiking, kayaking, trail running or skiing depending on the season.

Feel free to get in touch with me via one of the channels if you feel like a chat.
