MUSEUM SERVICE DESIGN CONCEPT, 2023

Figma Prototype / UX Design / Service Design / User Flow


When visiting a museum or gallery, we often seek out pieces by artists, religions, or movements we already know—basing our experience on the familiar. Our research, including interviews with museum visitors, has shown that having prior knowledge of a museum's collection can provide valuable context for the overall exhibition. Over the course of six weeks, we developed a service design concept which was presented to the Museum Rietberg.


Collaborators: Lukman Aščić, Matilda Sutherland, Audrey Lohmann, Plengpai Ratnajarn, Nanthatchaporn Pree Janthasom

ELASTIC ACOUSTIC, 2023

Sonic Interaction / Installation / Acoustics / New Interfaces


Elastic Acoustic is a textile-based interface which attempts to translate the movement of fabric into sound. Using contact microphones and movement sensors, the audience’s touch affects the sound source - the low rumbling of the recorded material sounds are amplified as the tube is enlarged, and the microphone output becomes more visceral with increased pressure.


Collaborators: Sonja Cowley, Loïc Himmel, and Mo Bünzli

EAPS, 2022

Arduino / C++ / Wearable Technology / Interaction Design


The EAPS headband extends our senses and allows us to feel what we cannot usually see, by inflating with decreases in air quality to apply gentle pressure to the users head. There is potential to be used by tradesmen working with dangerous fumes and toxic materials, acting as a warning built into their hard hat.


Collaborators: Lukman Aščić and Tanja Landolt

ABOUT

Drawing on a background in sound design and performance, I like to play with the potential for performance through code, and our interactions with digital environments.

I was born on the East Coast of Australia, with music and nature playing a big role in my childhood. After graduating high school, I made the move to the Big City and, while the fast-paced environment was exciting, I craved the stillness of coastal life. I believe we can find this with the interfaces that surround us, in simplicity, ease-of-use, and our understanding of how they function.

My interests lie in the future of human-computer interaction, and my skills cover web development, UX/UI, interaction and sound design.
