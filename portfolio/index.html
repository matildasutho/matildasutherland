PROJECTS

ELASTIC ACOUSTIC, 2023

Sonic Interaction / Installation / Acoustics / New Interfaces


Elastic Acoustic as an audio interface of sorts, a textile-based midi controller which attempts to translate the movement of fabric into sound. Using contact microphones and movement sensors, the audience’s touch affects the sound source - the low rumbling of the recorded material sounds are amplified as the tube is enlarged, and the microphone output becomes more visceral with increased pressure.

EAPS, 2022

Arduino / C++ / Wearable Technology / Interaction Design


The EAPS headband extends our senses and allows us to feel what we cannot usually see, by inflating with decreases in air quality to apply gentle pressure to the users head. There is potential to be used by tradesmen working with dangerous fumes and toxic materials, acting as a warning built into their hard hat.

MY LOVE LANGUAGE IS DIGITAL, 2021

Prototype / Installation / Senses / Interaction Design


With the rise of the digital sphere as a place to seek connection and romantic relationships and the associated shame we often feel over meeting partners online, comes the need to dissect the imposed binary of digital vs. IRL actions of love. There is an opportunity create an installation which explores what love looks and feels like for each individual, and promotes discourse on the topic. My Love Language Is... will include a sonic experience, and explore spatiality through installation. The result will be a user imbued by inquiry of love, specifically in the context of the digital and IRL romantic binary.
