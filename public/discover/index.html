Thinking about the current lack of sonic exchange through travel, this series of Girl On Road transmissions explores our potential to “discover” music virtually - in an attempt to emulate an experience of virtual discovery and to synthesise a sense of cross-cultural xenogamy. Each episode originally aired digitally via Skylab Radio. This website is the home to discover all forthcoming episodes of G.O.R..
