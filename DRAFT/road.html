BeforeI embark I must put on my shoes
Slid onto my foot, laces still fastened from the previous day
The less often I retie them the less solid they feel on my foot
But there's something about keeping the laces in tact which reminds me that my journey is not over
One day I'll get a pair of boots, then the journey will never end
